know about some places before your travel

Tourist Cities

Casablanca

Morocco's modern and cosmopolitan hub, famous for the stunning Hassan II Mosque by the sea. Enjoy its beachfront Corniche, stylish cafés, and art-deco buildings.

Chefchaouen

The Blue City nestled in the Rif Mountains. Famous for its blue-painted streets, calm vibe, and stunning mountain views — perfect for relaxing and taking photos.

Dades Valley

A dramatic valley with red rock canyons, ancient kasbahs, and scenic mountain roads. Ideal for hiking, photography, and discovering Berber villages.

Fes

The spiritual and cultural heart of Morocco. Its ancient medina, tanneries, and Al Quaraouiyine University make it a living museum of Moroccan heritage.

Marrakech

The Red City is Morocco's cultural gem, full of life, colors, and history. Explore Jemaa El-Fna, vibrant souks, and stunning gardens and palaces. A perfect mix of tradition and modern energy.

Rabat

The capital city of Morocco, combining modern life with history. Visit Hassan Tower, Kasbah of the Udayas, and enjoy the peaceful Atlantic coast atmosphere.
